#Thirdspacedigital notes

https://3rdspacedigital.org/

link this website.

The website was made with the intention of connecting people through media and real life events. We wanted to build a space at dallas where there we felt a lack of a third space. We exist as an archive and a living collective of people who wish to live life with community.

I used react for the website and designed it on figma pages. We build the backend on supabase, where we have all of the photo archives. 

I researched countless magazine editorial websites such as 032c.com to get the feel of our website. we were inspired by Ubisoft and analog y2k aesthetics.
